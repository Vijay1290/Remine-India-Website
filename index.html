What we do

The metal recycling industry is growing rapidly. At Remine, we work by always following a completely isolated process.

Collection

Collecting metals from cars, steel structures, ships, railroad tracks, farm equipment, and construction sites.

Sorting

Use industrial magnets to separate metals as ferrous and non-ferrous.

Crushing

Use numerous compactors to break down the materials to smaller, more manageable sizes.

Shredding or Granulating

In order to properly melt the metal, the material must be very small, so shredders are used to cut them into smaller pieces.

Melting and purification

Apply various melting and purification methods to the shredded material.

Solidifying

Cooling the metal is the final step, as it solidifies the metal into a working material..
